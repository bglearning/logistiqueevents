About Us

  • Who we are

    We are a group of youth striving to change the trend of how events are organized in Nepal. People and organizations alike usually struggle when it comes to organizing events effectively as they have more important work to focus on. So, a better option would be to let a group who specialize in managing events do it. That's where we come in.

  • What we do

    We take care of all the hassles you have to go through when organizing successful events -- from sleak corporate events to fun birthday bashes, from memorable product launchs to awesome road shows. With us working on your event, you don't have to make hundreds of phone calls to different stakeholders. You can just sit back and enjoy your cup of coffee.

  • So, basically

    Basically, we are like some superhero or maybe the sidekick who is fun-loving, lively and just great to hang out with but can also be serious and efficient at getting the things done. Usually, we are both at the same time.
